À Propos

Bloody Rock'n'roll from Charleroi, BE. Fed by bands stamped with a "Black" that set the tone, DANDY SHOES is a vintage spirit formation where dirty fuzz collides with muddy and verby sounds.

DANDY SHOES is a belgian Rock band consisting of Allan Berger (guitar & vocals), Romain Dumoulin (drums), Antoine Chauvaux (bass) and Arnaud Lietor Torres (guitar). Each with a distinct musical universe, the result gives a mixture of influences in a burst of shades such as , , , ... and many others.

"The dandy must aspire to be sublime without interruption" Baudelaire.

After a price at the contest "l'Envol des Cités", the group records their first LP "" with Roland De Greef () under Moonzoo Music label. Coming out in 2014, the record travels through vintage music with a modern touch.

With the arrival of "" the second album released in early 2019, the pop side fades away... It gives way to a more assertive and liberating Blues Rock. More natural and fulfilling for them. Still in this quest for Rock'n'Roll.

Pure energy, frenzied game scenes, "dandystic" attitude... This is what to expect from DANDY SHOES !
